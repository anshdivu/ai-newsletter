Feature Spotlight
This week
Plugins, capability you install once and carry everywhere
A plugin is one package that bundles skills, agents, hooks, MCP servers, even LSP config. Everything arrives namespaced as /plugin-name:skill, so nothing collides. Anthropic runs two public marketplaces: a curated official one, registered automatically on first launch, and a community catalog that reviews third-party submissions. Reach for plugins when you keep copying the same skills and hooks between projects, or when your whole team should share one setup.
